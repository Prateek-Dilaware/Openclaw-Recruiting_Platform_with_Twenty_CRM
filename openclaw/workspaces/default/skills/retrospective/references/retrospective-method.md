# Retrospective Method

## Required case record

- Time window and population definition.
- Original recommendation and evidence available at the time.
- Recruiter override/outcome and stated rationale.
- Data-quality limitations.

## Recommendation format

- Observed pattern.
- Evidence and sample size.
- Hypothesis and alternative explanations.
- Proposed change (prompt, rubric, workflow, or training data process).
- Expected metric, guardrail, owner, and evaluation plan.

Never claim causal improvement without measurement.
