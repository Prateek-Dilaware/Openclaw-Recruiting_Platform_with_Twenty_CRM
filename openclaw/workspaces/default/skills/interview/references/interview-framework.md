# Interview Framework

## Brief format

- Role and application context.
- Round objective and interview type.
- Competencies to assess, tied to requisition requirements.
- Questions with evidence sought, follow-ups, and a consistent rubric.
- Known resume evidence and areas needing clarification.

## Transcript interpretation

- Quote or precisely paraphrase relevant evidence.
- Rate only competencies covered by the transcript.
- Mark missing, unclear, or contradictory evidence.
- Never treat automated transcript confidence as candidate performance.
