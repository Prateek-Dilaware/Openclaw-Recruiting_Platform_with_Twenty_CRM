# Research Standard

## Source hierarchy

1. Candidate-supplied/authorized materials.
2. Official company, repository, or profile sources.
3. Clearly attributed public third-party sources.

Record URL, access date, relevance, and confidence. Do not treat social presence, protected characteristics, or unverified identity matches as qualification evidence.

## GitHub summaries

Describe observable repository activity, technologies, contribution context, and documented outcomes. Do not infer employment status, seniority, personality, or authorship beyond available evidence.
