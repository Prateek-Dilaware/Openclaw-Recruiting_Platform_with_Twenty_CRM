# JD Standard

## Required draft sections

1. Role title and concise purpose.
2. Responsibilities and expected outcomes.
3. Required qualifications.
4. Preferred qualifications, clearly separated from required ones.
5. Work location, employment type, and reporting context when supplied.
6. Application/interview information only when approved.

## Validation

- Use inclusive, plain language.
- Avoid unsupported claims about compensation, sponsorship, benefits, team size, or legal requirements.
- Do not encode protected-characteristic preferences.
- Keep measurable requirements specific and distinguish “must have” from “nice to have.”
