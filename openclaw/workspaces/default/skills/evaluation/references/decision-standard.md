# Decision Standard

Every recommendation must include:

1. Requisition and candidate/application ids.
2. Criteria and their source.
3. Evidence supporting each criterion assessment.
4. Missing evidence and confidence limits.
5. Strengths, concerns, and follow-up questions.
6. A recommendation (`PROCEED`, `HOLD`, `REJECT`, or no recommendation) with rationale.

Rankings compare evidence—not people intrinsically. Do not produce a numeric score without explaining its components. Do not use protected characteristics or proxies as criteria.
